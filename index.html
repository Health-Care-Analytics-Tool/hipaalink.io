How HIPAA Link works

1
Capture
Your CRM or practice system tracks milestones - lead, booked exam, start, revenue. We connect to your existing workflow without changing it.

3
Send Signals That Improve Ads
Platforms match anonymous hashed events to their own logs and learn which actions lead to valuable outcomes - without seeing any person.
4
Optimize & Scale
Real attribution, smarter bidding, and lower acquisition costs - your marketing finally performs like modern performance marketing, safely.
